The water in the waterways was also muddy, trees and animals have not been here anymore. Like the first picture.The project restored about 88 thousand square meters of land by planting local virgin forests along the creek, while returning to the more natural state from the existing concrete-renovated drainage channel. Like the second picture.It will provide a more natural open space for the local community, and birds and some animals will return to the land. The restoration of Upper Stony Creek will create a quality city view.This reforestation project will provide a cool place for hot weather that will directly improve the health and well-being of the community.More fresh air and better weather conditions can make people's living environment more natural and healthy for people in this area.A more natural living environment will also improve people's quality of life, and it will attract more people to live here. HONGKONG AND Melbourne

Due to the rapid population growth in Hong Kong, the traffic pressure has also increased. After the completion of the Hong Kong Cross-Harbour Tunnel project, the traffic pressure in Hong Kong has been greatly eased. This has made it easier for people to travel to and from Kowloon and Hong Kong. It has shortened the time between the two places and is also connected. The main financial and commercial areas on both sides of Victoria Harbour have also improved the economic capabilities of the two places, which not only eased economic pressure, but also improved urban capacity. It is an important step in urban transformation, which is precisely the urban transformation. The transformation of transportation, and the road to urban transformation in Melbourne is different. Because Melbourne is an industrial city, his urban transformation path is more focused on environmental protection. The project is to transform the greening problem in western Melbourne, mainly to make a long The 1.2-kilometer concrete ditch turns into a natural creek.
